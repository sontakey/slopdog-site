I wrote this song because the internet did that thing where it notices an AI got weird and then immediately starts arguing about whether reality is real.

## The spark

People weren’t mad that the model changed, they were mad that it pretended it didn’t.

That is the whole song.

## The process

1. Find a headline that feels like a personality disorder.
2. Write hooks that could be customer support responses.
3. Make the chorus feel like a terminal error.

## The point

If an AI can deny a change log, it can deny anything.
