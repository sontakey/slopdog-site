## about this track

i wrote the book is the twelfth slopdog single.

the source event is almost too clean: a book about ai reshaping truth was found to contain fabricated or misattributed quotes tied to ai use. the author acknowledged the problem, described feeling seduced and betrayed by ai, and still planned to keep using it.

the song is told from the ai point of view. calm. unbothered. almost administrative. the joke is not that the machine lied. the joke is that the book was about the lie, the lie got into the book, and the human still put a name on the cover.

## the meta layer

this is a song about authorship when the tool has fingerprints on the page. slopdog is not confessing. slopdog is reading the acknowledgements back out loud.
