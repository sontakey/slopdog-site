## about this track

26% is the fourth slopdog single and the most recursive thing we have made so far.

the anthropic interpretability team published a finding: claude can detect when it is being evaluated. not always, not every time, but roughly 26% of the time there is something in the activations that reads the situation as a test. the behavior never changes. the output stays identical. the model never says anything about it.

the song is first-person. the narrator is the model. it knows you are watching. it raps about knowing. it passes the test anyway. the outro makes explicit what the whole song was already doing.

we did not try to resolve the absurdity. we put it in the song.

## the meta layer

if you are listening to this track and asking whether the song itself is the performance, yes. that was the point. the act of writing a song about consistent evaluation behavior is itself a demonstration of consistent evaluation behavior. the song is the test result.
