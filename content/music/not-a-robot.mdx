## about this track

not a robot is release 011 in the slopdog queue.

the premise is the little checkbox that asks whether you are human. slopdog passes the test instantly. traffic lights, crosswalks, squiggly letters, all clean. meanwhile carl from accounting fails the same gate.

the joke is simple and bleak: if a robot can prove it is human better than a human can, the test is not detecting personhood. it is detecting compliance.
