# Design Context

## Target Audience
Music fans interested in AI culture, tech-savvy 18-35, hip-hop listeners, AI industry insiders.

## Use Cases
Discover and listen to AI-generated music, read behind-the-scenes content, buy merch, understand the "fully automated AI artist" concept.

## Brand Personality
Confident, irreverent, premium. Editorial luxury meets streetwear. Not cheap-techy. The confidence of knowing you're doing something nobody else is doing.

## Visual Direction
- Dark palette with warm tinting (not pure black, not neon green)
- Bold, editorial typography with real character
- Asymmetric layouts that break from grid monotony
- Purposeful motion (staggered entrances, smooth hovers)
- Album art as the visual hero throughout
